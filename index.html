Our Story

A Beautiful Space for Coffee & Connection

A cozy cafe in Surulere designed for relaxation, meaningful conversations, and great food — from waffles and wings to refreshing Zobo drinks.

Arami Cafe is more than just a coffee spot — it's a cozy sanctuary in Surulere, Lagos. Our space is perfect for all occasions, from casual gatherings to intimate dates. We serve carefully crafted drinks, satisfying bites, and a warm atmosphere that keeps guests coming back.

Known for our standout Zobo drink — a refreshing hibiscus and pineapple blend — and our hearty platters, Arami Cafe is your go-to cozy spot in Lagos.
